Since 2025

A small team, one workshop.

Remake works started with a single denim piece, sewn in a friend's living space. It's grown into a three-piece line, but the workshop, the mills, and the people checking each seam haven't changed.

Where it started

A pattern that didn't fit anything on the rack

The founder, Joe, realised that we can make clothes out of clothes. The first piece being a blue denim that he sat down and sewed from other piece making a masterpiece. This partly proved the idea, and once he showcased his piece, people were fascinated and interested in more.

Two years later, we work with two weaving mills and one cut-and-sew partner.

What we believe

Three rules we don't break

Repairable by design

Every piece ships with a repair card. Send it back and we'll mend it, free, for as long as you own it.

Made by trusted partners

Weaving, dyeing, and cut-and-sew all happen with partners we've visited in person, more than once.

Small, fixed batches

We cut to a planned quantity instead of forecasting demand, so there's no leftover stock to discount or discard.
