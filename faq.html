Help center

Frequently asked questions

Sizing, shipping, returns, and our repair program. Can't find it here? Ask us directly.

Orders within Nairobi ship within 2 business days. We don't currently offer expedited shipping, since most of our stock is cut to order in small batches.

Unworn items with tags attached can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Sale items and the Everyday Crew Socks are final sale for hygiene reasons. Email us through the contact page to start a return.

Every garment ships with a repair card printed with a unique code. If a seam fails or a button comes loose, send us a message with that code and a photo of the damage — we'll send a prepaid mailing label and repair it free of charge, with no time limit, for as long as you own the piece.

Yes, our Selvedge Straight Jean is unsanforized, so expect roughly 3–5% shrinkage in length after the first wash. We've already accounted for this in our size chart, so buy your usual size rather than sizing up.

Right now we only ship within Kenya. But we shall work to ship internationally soon
